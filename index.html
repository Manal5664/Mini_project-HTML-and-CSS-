Why We’re Different

  • The Secret Recipe: Our signature 18-spice secret blend is a guarded family recipe that delivers a flavor profile you won't find anywhere else.

  • The Legend of Toum: No meal is complete without our world-famous garlic sauce, the creamy, bold companion to every Albaik meal.

Dessert

A Symphony of Sweetness

No dining experience is truly complete without a decadent finale, and our dessert menu offers the perfect sweet ending to your Albaik journey. We have curated a selection of treats that balance rich textures with delicate flavors, from creamy puddings to moist, chocolate-infused delights. Each dessert is crafted using premium ingredients like high-quality cocoa, fresh dairy, and natural sweeteners to ensure a high-end taste. Whether you prefer something light and fruity or deep and indulgent, our sweets are designed to leave you with a smile. It is the crowning glory of our menu that keeps you coming back for one more bite.

Signature Appetizers

Pasta

Pasta: Italian Comfort with a Twist

Our pasta dishes are a tribute to the art of Italian cooking, bringing comfort and sophistication to your table in every bowl. We use only the finest durum wheat pasta, cooked strictly al dente to provide the perfect foundation for our rich, simmered-from-scratch sauces. Whether you crave the velvety smoothness of a garlic-infused Alfredo or the zesty kick of a herb-packed marinara, our chefs ensure every strand is perfectly coated. Finished with a dusting of aged parmesan and fresh basil, these dishes provide a warm, satisfying hug for your taste buds. It’s a refined dining experience that brings the heart of Italy to your plate.

Salad

Salads: Freshness in Every Forkful

Refresh your palate with our vibrant selection of salads, where garden-fresh ingredients take center stage in a colorful display of health and flavor. We source the crispest greens, crunchy cucumbers, and sun-sweetened peppers daily to ensure that every bowl is bursting with nutrients. Each salad is tossed with one of our handcrafted dressings, ranging from zesty vinaigrettes to creamy, herb-infused specialties. Topped with toasted nuts, seeds, or grilled protein, our salads are designed to be both a light side and a filling, energizing main course. It is the perfect choice for those seeking a crisp, clean, and delicious meal.

Burger

Burgers: The Ultimate Satisfaction

Our burgers are engineered for the perfect bite, starting with premium, hand-picked cuts of meat seasoned with our secret spice blend. Each patty is seared to perfection to lock in the juices, then layered with crisp lettuce, vine-ripened tomatoes, and our signature house sauces. We nestle everything inside a buttery, toasted brioche bun that holds up until the very last bite. Whether you prefer the heat of spicy jalapeños or the classic melt of cheddar cheese, our burgers offer a gourmet experience that redefines fast-casual dining. It is not just a sandwich; it’s a masterpiece of flavor and texture.

Pizza

Pizza: A Slice of Perfection

Experience the authentic taste of tradition combined with modern culinary innovation in every slice of our artisanal pizzas. Our dough is slow-fermented for 24 hours to achieve a light, airy crust that is perfectly charred in our high-heat ovens. We use a base of rich San Marzano tomato sauce and 100% whole-milk mozzarella to ensure a gooey, flavorful stretch every time. From traditional toppings to our bold, Albaik-inspired chicken toppings, every pizza is a celebration of fresh ingredients. It’s the perfect meal for sharing with friends or indulging in a solo feast of cheesy goodness.
